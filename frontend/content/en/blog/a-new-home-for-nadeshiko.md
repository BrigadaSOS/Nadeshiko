# A New Home for Nadeshiko

We're excited to announce that **Nadeshiko** has officially moved to its new home. After months of work behind the scenes, we've rebuilt the platform from the ground up to bring you a faster, more reliable, and more polished experience.

## What's New?

Here's a quick overview of the highlights:

- **New infrastructure** — The entire backend has been rebuilt for better performance and scalability.
- **Improved search** — Search results are now faster and more accurate than ever.
- **Better audio playback** — The audio player has been redesigned for a smoother experience.
- **User accounts** — You can now create an account to save your preferences and API keys.

## What's Coming Next?

We have a lot planned for the near future:

- **Pitch accent annotations** — Visual pitch accent markers directly on search results.
- **User media preferences** — Boost or hide specific anime from your search results.
- **AniList integration** — Import your anime list to personalize your experience.
- **More content** — We're continuously adding new anime to the library.

## Thank You

None of this would be possible without the community. Thank you to everyone who has supported Nadeshiko along the way — whether by using it, reporting bugs, suggesting features, or spreading the word.

We hope you enjoy the new Nadeshiko. If you have any questions or feedback, feel free to reach out on [Discord](https://discord.gg/ajWm26ADEj) or via email at contact@nadeshiko.co.

Happy searching!
